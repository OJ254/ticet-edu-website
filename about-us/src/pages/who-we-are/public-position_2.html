Academic Dishonesty

At Ticet EDU Limited, we strongly oppose academic dishonesty in all its forms, including plagiarism, cheating, and misrepresentation of work. We believe that integrity and originality are foundational to the learning process and essential for personal and academic growth. To uphold these values, we are committed to fostering a culture of honesty and ethical behavior among our users.

Our platform incorporates a range of tools and resources designed to create awareness about academic dishonesty and educate users on the importance of producing original content. We provide guidelines, tutorials, and interactive modules that teach users about the principles of intellectual honesty and the consequences of academic misconduct. Additionally, our platform features plagiarism detection tools that help users ensure their work is original and properly cited.

We also encourage a community-driven approach to academic integrity by promoting peer review and feedback mechanisms. By empowering users to create and share original content, we aim to cultivate an environment where academic honesty is valued and celebrated. At Ticet EDU, we are dedicated to supporting our users in developing the skills and mindset needed to succeed ethically in their academic and professional endeavors.

Competition

At Ticet EDU Limited, we believe that the edtech industry is a dynamic and rapidly evolving space with tremendous potential to transform education. We recognize that the market is largely untapped and can accommodate multiple local and international players, each contributing to the betterment of education by enhancing accessibility and quality. Rather than viewing other edtech solutions as competitors, we see them as partners in our shared mission to make learning more inclusive, engaging, and effective for all.

Ticet EDU positions itself as a facilitator within the edtech ecosystem. Our platform is designed to be open and collaborative, allowing us to integrate and onboard third-party edtech solutions seamlessly. By providing a space where various educational tools and resources can coexist, we aim to create a holistic learning environment that caters to diverse needs and learning styles.

We believe that fostering a collaborative approach in the edtech industry will drive innovation, improve educational outcomes, and provide learners worldwide with the best possible opportunities. At Ticet EDU, we are committed to working alongside other edtech companies to empower educators and learners, ultimately contributing to a brighter future for education globally.

Privacy & Data Protection

At Ticet EDU Limited, user privacy and data protection are paramount. We understand that safeguarding personal information is a top concern for our users, and we are committed to addressing these concerns with the highest standards of security and transparency.

Our platform employs robust measures to ensure your data is protected from unauthorized access and breaches. We use advanced encryption techniques, anonymization, and pseudonymization to secure your personal data both in transit and at rest. Our data processing practices are in strict compliance with data protection laws, including the Data Protection Act, 2019 (Kenya).

We are transparent about our data collection practices, only collecting information necessary to provide and improve our services. Users have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of their personal data at any time. We ensure that data is retained only for as long as needed and securely deleted when no longer required.

Our privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data, and we regularly review and update it to reflect changes in laws and best practices. By prioritizing your privacy and implementing stringent data protection measures, Ticet EDU aims to create a secure and trustworthy environment for all our users.

For more details on our data protection and privacy practices, please refer to our Privacy Policy and Data Protection Policy .

Education for All

At Ticet EDU Limited, we firmly believe that education is a powerful equalizer and a fundamental right that should be accessible and affordable to everyone. We are dedicated to breaking down barriers to education by providing a platform that supports inclusive and equitable learning opportunities for all individuals, regardless of their background or financial status.

By leveraging technology and innovative solutions, Ticet EDU strives to make high-quality education accessible and affordable, reaching learners in diverse geographical and socio-economic contexts.

Collaboration is central to achieving this vision. We actively partner with educational institutions, content creators, and non-profit organizations to enhance our platform's offerings and extend its reach. Through these collaborations, we aim to enrich our educational resources and support systems, ensuring that all learners have the tools and opportunities they need to succeed.

Ticet EDU is committed to promoting lifelong learning and educational equity, contributing to a global movement towards a more inclusive and informed society.
