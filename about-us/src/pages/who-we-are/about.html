Who we are

Ticet EDU Limited is an innovative edtech company dedicated to revolutionizing collaborative learning through its platform, Ticet EDU . We provide a dedicated space for educators, leaners, schools, and other stakeholders in the education sector to connect, share resources, and engage in meaningful educational projects. Our approach is centered on harnessing the power of social media and technology to enhance education and personalize learning experiences for learners and support educators. We are committed to diversity, equity, and inclusion, ensuring that all users have access to quality education tailored to their needs. Our stance on user data emphasizes privacy and security, ensuring a safe learning environment. As advocates for competency-based education and anti-plagiarism, we aim to uphold academic integrity while promoting sustainable, inclusive education for all. Ticet EDU is dedicated to fostering a culture of learning that is accessible, equitable, and innovative.

The Company

Ticet EDU Limited, founded in 2024 in Nairobi, Kenya, is an edtech company focused on revolutionizing education through its LXP, Ticet EDU . It offers secure, collaborative learning experiences with diverse educational resources and advanced features like AI assistants and personalized learning.

Our Position

At Ticet EDU, we carefully considered and deeply held positions on various aspects of education and technology. We advocate for responsible use of social media and the internet in education, providing a safe and controlled environment for collaborative learning. We embrace the potential of artificial intelligence to personalize learning experiences and optimize teaching outcomes. We believe in the transformative power of technology to make education more accessible and affordable. We promote diversity, equity, inclusion, and collaboration in education while emphasizing academic integrity and ethical practices to combat plagiarism and academic dishonesty.

What is a Learning Experience Platform (LXP)?

A Learning Experience Platform (LXP) offers a personalized, interactive learning experience that goes beyond traditional educational methods. With an LXP, students can connect, collaborate, and access diverse resources tailored to their unique learning needs. By fostering social learning, community engagement, and seamless access to educational content, LXPs are reshaping how students approach education in today’s digital age.
