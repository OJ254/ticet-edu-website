Our Impact

Ticet EDU is committed to a sustainable, socially responsible, and ethically governed platform that supports collaborative learning in ed-tech. Through environmental initiatives, we reduce our carbon footprint by optimizing digital operations, embracing green hosting, and promoting eco-awareness among users. Our social impact aims to make quality education accessible to all by adopting a sharing economy model, fostering economic empowerment for educators through income opportunities, and creating a collaborative community where users can engage and share knowledge. In governance, we uphold high standards of transparency, data privacy, and ethical practices, ensuring a secure, accountable, and fair platform for all stakeholders. These principles shape Ticet EDU’s approach to building a supportive, impactful learning environment, driving positive change in education while adhering to sustainable and responsible practices across all aspects of our operations.

Triple Bottom Line

At Ticet EDU, we are deeply committed to integrating Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) values into our operations. We believe in sustainable growth that aligns with global efforts to reduce environmental impact, promote social equity, and maintain ethical governance. Through responsible resource usage, inclusive education initiatives, and transparent practices, Ticet EDU strives to make a positive difference in the lives of our users and the communities we serve. Our commitment to ESG reflects our dedication to creating lasting, positive change through technology and education.

Environment

Ticet EDU is dedicated to reducing its environmental impact through sustainable digital practices. We prioritize digital sustainability by minimizing our platform's carbon footprint, using efficient hosting solutions, and exploring renewable energy options. By optimizing resource efficiency, we ensure minimal waste in energy consumption, coding practices, and data management, creating a more eco-friendly and cost-effective platform. Our commitment also includes environmental awareness and education—we collaborate with eco-conscious educators to provide resources on environmental issues, promoting sustainability within our community. Through these initiatives, Ticet EDU aims to foster a responsible digital environment and inspire our users to adopt eco-friendly practices, reflecting our dedication to a sustainable future in ed-tech.

Society

Ticet EDU strives for inclusive access to education, ensuring that quality learning resources are affordable and accessible to all through a sharing economy model. We empower educators by providing income opportunities through both paid and public libraries, fostering economic empowerment and professional growth. With community engagement and collaboration at our core, Ticet EDU enables meaningful interactions, feedback exchanges, and knowledge-sharing among users, creating a collaborative and supportive learning ecosystem. These efforts ensure that both learners and educators are active participants in a diverse, inclusive community. Ticet EDU is committed to making a positive social impact by supporting educators, fostering engagement, and promoting equal access to knowledge.

Governance

Ticet EDU values transparency and accountability as essential components of our governance. We are committed to ethical, open communication and policies that build trust with users, educators, and stakeholders. Data privacy and security are paramount, with robust protection measures, industry best practices, and user control over personal information. Additionally, Ticet EDU prioritizes ethical business practices across all operations, from content moderation to partnerships, ensuring fairness, respect, and inclusivity. Our commitment to these governance principles reinforces our mission to be a trusted, responsible ed-tech platform, providing a secure and respectful environment where users can learn, share, and grow.

A Note from Ticet EDU's CEO

At Ticet EDU, our commitment to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) values is not just a priority; it’s part of our foundation. As an alumnus of the Tony Elumelu Foundation, I have learned the impact of sustainable and responsible business, and I’m proud to bring this approach to Ticet EDU. Our goal is to empower communities, create inclusive learning opportunities, and ensure transparency in every aspect of our operations.

Through our Community Library, the first of many innovations, we’ve created a platform rooted in collaboration and accessibility. While Ticet EDU is subscription-based, our sharing economy model lowers costs for learners by enabling educators to create both premium and communal resources. Verified content creators benefit from active income from paid libraries or passive income from revenue-sharing on public libraries. This approach ensures that users can access a wide range of quality educational resources at affordable rates, fostering a supportive ecosystem where all members benefit.

Environmental stewardship is also central to our journey. We’re mindful of reducing our carbon footprint, utilizing sustainable digital practices, and minimizing waste in all operations. Our vision for the future includes supporting green technologies as we grow.

Transparency and accountability are at the core of our governance practices. By establishing clear and ethical policies, we ensure trust among our users, partners, and stakeholders, fostering a supportive and resilient community. Together, we can build an ed-tech platform that positively impacts lives, society, and the planet.
