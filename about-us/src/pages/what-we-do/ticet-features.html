What we Do

Ticet EDU is a collaborative learning platform focused on empowering educators and learners alike. Our MVP, the Community Library, is a hub where verified educators can create and share diverse learning materials—accessible to learners through private, protected, paid, or public libraries. Integrated social tools enable users to discuss content, engage with creators, and build communities around shared learning goals. Collaborative tools make it simple for educators and learners to connect and share knowledge, while our ed-tech tools support content creation, personalization, and accessibility. As a flexible, user-driven space, Ticet EDU bridges the gap between traditional learning and digital innovation. We’re continuously developing new features to expand and enhance users' experiences, and fostering a richer, more engaging educational journey.

Features

Ticet EDU, as a Learning Experience Platform (LXP), offers key features like collaborative tools for group projects and discussions, social tools for peer-to-peer engagement and community building, and edtech tools that support personalized learning, interactive lessons, and real-time progress tracking, enhancing the overall learning experience.

For Learners

Ticet EDU offers a comprehensive value proposition to learners by integrating collaborative, social, and edtech tools into a seamless Learning Experience Platform (LXP). Our platform empowers students through peer-to-peer learning, fostering collaboration and community building. With features like online discussions, group projects, and a global network of learners, students can engage deeply with content and develop essential skills beyond the classroom.

For Educators

Ticet EDU offers a compelling value proposition to content creators, including industry professionals, teachers, and lecturers, by providing a dynamic platform that enhances teaching, networking, and monetization opportunities. With collaborative tools, creators can connect with peers, collaborate on projects, and learn from other educators and professionals, enriching their teaching practices and expanding their influence.
