Our Commitment to ESG Excellence

Empowering communities, driving sustainable innovation, and fostering transparency through dedicated Environmental, Social, and Governance principles.

At Ticet Edu, we are deeply committed to integrating Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) values into our operations. We believe in sustainable growth that aligns with global efforts to reduce environmental impact, promote social equity, and maintain ethical governance. Through responsible resource usage, inclusive education initiatives, and transparent practices, Ticet Edu strives to make a positive difference in the lives of our users and the communities we serve.

Our commitment to ESG reflects our dedication to creating lasting, positive change through technology and education.

A Note from Ticet Edu's CEO

At Ticet Edu, our commitment to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) values is not just a priority; it’s part of our foundation. As an alumnus of the Tony Elumelu Foundation, I have learned the impact of sustainable and responsible business, and I’m proud to bring this approach to Ticet Edu. Our goal is to empower communities, create inclusive learning opportunities, and ensure transparency in every aspect of our operations.

Through our Community Library, the first of many innovations, we’ve created a platform rooted in collaboration and accessibility. While Ticet Edu is subscription-based, our sharing economy model lowers costs for learners by enabling educators to create both premium and communal resources. Verified content creators benefit from active income from paid libraries or passive income from revenue-sharing on public libraries. This approach ensures that users can access a wide range of quality educational resources at affordable rates, fostering a supportive ecosystem where all members benefit.

Environmental stewardship is also central to our journey. We’re mindful of reducing our carbon footprint, utilizing sustainable digital practices, and minimizing waste in all operations. Our vision for the future includes supporting green technologies as we grow.

Transparency and accountability are at the core of our governance practices. By establishing clear and ethical policies, we ensure trust among our users, partners, and stakeholders, fostering a supportive and resilient community. Together, we can build an ed-tech platform that positively impacts lives, society, and the planet.

Japheth Oruko

CO-FOUNDER & Chief Executive Officer
