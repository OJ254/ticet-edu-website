Who we are

Public Position

Ticet Edu Limited is dedicated to fostering academic integrity, promoting sustainability, and advancing diversity, equity, and inclusion in education. We oppose academic dishonesty and offer tools and resources to help users create original content, supporting a culture of honesty. In the edtech industry, we view competition as an opportunity for collaboration, aiming to integrate and enhance various educational tools to improve accessibility and quality. Our commitment to user privacy and data protection is unwavering; we use advanced encryption and adhere to strict compliance with data protection laws to safeguard personal information. Sustainability is central to our operations, as we prioritize eco-friendly practices and social responsibility, working to reduce our carbon footprint and enhance educational equity. We embrace diversity and inclusion by fostering a supportive workplace, partnering with diverse suppliers and content creators, and providing continuous DEI training. Our belief in "Education for All" aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 4, striving to make quality education accessible and affordable globally. Through these efforts, Ticet Edu aims to drive positive change, empower learners, and contribute to a more inclusive and sustainable educational landscape.

Academic Dishonesty

We strongly oppose academic dishonesty in all its forms, including plagiarism, cheating, and misrepresentation of work. We believe that integrity and originality are foundational to the learning process and essential for personal and academic growth. To uphold these values, we are committed to fostering a culture of honesty and ethical behavior among our users.

Competition

We believe that the edtech industry is a dynamic and rapidly evolving space with tremendous potential to transform education. We recognize that the market is largely untapped and can accommodate multiple local and international players, each contributing to the betterment of education by enhancing accessibility and quality. Rather than viewing other edtech solutions as competitors, we see them as partners in our shared mission to make learning more inclusive, engaging, and effective for all.

Privacy & Data Protection

At Ticet Edu Limited, user privacy and data protection are paramount. We understand that safeguarding personal information is a top concern for our users, and we are committed to addressing these concerns with the highest standards of security and transparency.

Sustainability

Sustainability is integral to our mission. We are committed to creating a positive impact on both the environment and society through responsible practices and innovative solutions. Our approach to sustainability encompasses environmental stewardship, resource efficiency, and social responsibility.

Diversity, Equity, & Inclusion

At Ticet Edu Limited, Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) are fundamental to our culture and operations. We are dedicated to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace where every individual, regardless of background, identity, or perspective, feels valued and empowered.

Education for All

We firmly believe that education is a powerful equalizer and a fundamental right that should be accessible and affordable to everyone. We are dedicated to breaking down barriers to education by providing a platform that supports inclusive and equitable learning opportunities for all individuals, regardless of their background or financial status.
